FAMILY DENTISTRY MADE EASY

Maintaining good dental hygiene is an important step in having an overall clean bill of health. Unfortunately, many patients suffer from dentophobia, or the fear of going to the dentist. Robert D. Aufrichtig, D.M.D. is ready to erase your worries through exceptional dental services for you and your family! 

Located in Mt. Kisco, NY, Robert D. Aufrichtig, D.M.D. is dedicated to listening, understanding, and responding to our patients’ dental concerns. Our entire staff utilizes a gentle, compassionate approach to help ease your anxieties. 

Not only do we offer cosmetic dental services, such as teeth whitening and dental veneers, we also offer general dentistry services for adults and children alike. 

We are currently accepting new patients and look forward to meeting you! Give us a call today to make an appointment!

ABOUT OUR PRACTICE IN MT. KISCO, NY

Dr. Rob has been in practice for the last 30 years! Developments in technology and procedures have certainly changed in that time, but our personalized and caring approach to both children and adults has stayed the same.

Your dental health is far too important to sacrifice. We understand the stress that is often associated with a trip to the dentist, and it’s our job to create an environment where our patients can realize that they don’t have to be afraid! Through our techniques and quality care, we’re confident that you’ll only have positive memories of the dentist from here on. We offer general, pediatric, cosmetic, and stress-free dentistry services for our patients throughout Mt. Kisco, NY. Contact us today!
